About

Pulse Literary & Arts Magazine is an annual multimedia literary and arts magazine led by students at Point Park University.

Mission

We seek submissions that draw meaning from our world and the intricacies of our human experience across literary and visual forms.

Editor's Note

"At Pulse, we believe every voice has a story worth telling. Our mission is to amplify the creative spirit of Point Park University and beyond, creating a space where art and literature converge to inspire, challenge, and connect us all."

- Pulse Editorial Team
